vijay

Vijay, also known as Thalapathy Vijay, is a leading Indian actor and playback singer who predominantly works in Tamil cinema. Renowned for his charismatic screen presence and versatile acting skills, Vijay has starred in numerous blockbuster films and has a massive fan following across South India. His notable movies include "Ghilli," "Thuppakki," "Mersal," and "Master." Vijay is celebrated for his energetic performances, dance moves, and philanthropic activities, making him one of the most influential and beloved stars in the industry.
